LA Weekly  ·  April 7, 2026  ·  Nia Bowers

Peter Kleinhans Maps the Modern Condition on "Selections"

"As 'Selections' unfolds, it reveals Peter Kleinhans as a deeply attentive observer — his perspective moving from horses to markets to music and ultimately to the unease of watching a culture shift as the world becomes just a little too different, too fast."

Kleinhans examines technological anxiety, modern desire, and the struggle to remain human as algorithms are designed to make us more predictable. His sound moves fluidly between pop and folk rock, with guitar work that leans into jazz fusion and a warm, distinctive voice that has drawn comparisons to Dave Matthews.
